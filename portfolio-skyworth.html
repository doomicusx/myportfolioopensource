Challenge

This was a complete project that I started from scratch — from creating the name and the brand to developing the entire site . Even though it was a study project, I wanted to take it seriously and put everything I know into practice. The biggest challenge was juggling several roles at once: design, content, development, animations, and illustrations . In addition, some ideas I wanted to implement ended up being difficult to accomplish, which required a lot of adaptation .

SOLUTION

I took on all the stages by myself, testing different approaches to turn the ideas into something functional and visually interesting . Even with technical obstacles, I adjusted the project so it would maintain consistency and quality , always focusing on a modern and strategic design. It was an intense learning process that helped me broaden my skills in several areas at the same time.

DELIVERY

In the end, I delivered a complete project that clearly shows my style, dedication, and ability to tackle a big challenge on my own. It’s a piece that reflects my desire to create something unique, pushing boundaries and learning in practice.
