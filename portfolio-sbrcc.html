Challenge

The mission was to create a landing page for SBRCC, a medical society that needed to present its information in a clear, organized way and with a premium look. The project was done in partnership with Barbas Digital, Lepa Roscini and the copywriter Tadeu Brettas. The biggest challenge was dealing with the limitations of the tool used in the project (Uncode), which greatly restricted what was possible in terms of layout and interactivity. In addition, the design needed to maintain a sophisticated feel, without sacrificing clarity and functionality.

SOLUTION

I made the most of what the tool allowed, using small visual solutions and strategic adjustments to create a fluid and elegant experience. Tadeu’s well-structured content helped a lot, and communication with the client was direct , which made it easier to propose new ideas. I also created AI generative images to complement the visuals, maintaining the premium identity without relying on external resources.

DELIVERY

The result was a clean, functional website with an above-expected aesthetic, even with the limitations. I like this project precisely because of that: I managed to deliver something consistent and with personality, even with little room for experimentation. It’s a good example of how to adapt ideas without losing quality.
